History

Kunthala (pronounced: kun-THA-la) is a vassal state of Mahishmati and the residence of Devasena, Jaya Varma, Jaya Varma's Unnamed Wife, and Kumara Varma. The kingdom has bulls and cows as its mascots and is known for advanced agriculture. It was destroyed by Bhallaladeva during a conquest. Previously known as Kadarimandalam, it was won by Sivagami and Mahadeva. Kunthala was later given to the Vaitalika tribe as a gesture of peace. However, the kingdom fell into ruin after Bhallaladeva's conquest.
